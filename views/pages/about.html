About

I'm Aman. A Software Engineer, Developer and Designer.

The cusp of tehcnology and design has always fascinated me and I've never been afraid to just jump in and give it a go, whether it's Programming, Development, Robotics or Photoshop. I’ve been Developing with computers since the day I first opened console.

As a Full Stack Developer and System Designer, I thrive on the challenge of integrating front-end and back-end technologies to create cohesive, scalable solutions. My expertise spans across a variety of areas, including Web Development, API design, Database Management, and Cloud Architecture. This diverse skill set allows me to build robust applications that not only look great but also perform efficiently.

What excites me most about being a Software Developer is being able to Develop and create things that have purpose and solve real problems. It goes beyond Developing tools and websites and involves having a passion for developing experiences and solutions that help people, whether it's helping them make better products, market themselves online, or buy something online. Leaning into customer insight and understanding their needs, finding the right problems to solve, delivering solutions as quickly as possible, learning from those and then iterating and improving that value over time is the key to great software developer.

This is one of my favourite quotes (by Steve Jobs):

“Everyone here has the sense that right now is one of those moments when we are influencing the future.”
This resonates so deeply with me because I have no desire to make pretty things just for the sake of it. Some people love that! But it's not for me. I only want to make, develop or build ideas and products that are real, that matter and have an impact in the world.
